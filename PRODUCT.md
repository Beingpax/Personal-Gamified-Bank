# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People using a local-first personal reward tracker to turn daily effort into visible progress, target milestones, wheel spins, and earned rewards. They are usually updating a quick daily total, checking whether the next milestone is close, or managing the rewards that can appear on the wheel.

## Product Purpose

Personal Bank helps users convert arbitrary daily effort scores into motivating reward moments. Success means the user can understand their current total, the next target, available spins, and reward history without needing to think about real finance, account balances, or budgets.

## Brand Personality

Practical, encouraging, playful. The interface should feel like a focused tool with a satisfying reward loop, not a finance dashboard or a marketing page.

## Anti-references

Do not make the app feel like banking, investing, crypto, or budget-planning software. Do not disrupt the current spin wheel experience. Avoid decorative dashboard clutter that makes the daily log, target progress, or reward history harder to scan.

## Design Principles

- Make progress legible at a glance.
- Keep the reward loop visible from setup through claimed history.
- Treat money values as game scores, not financial data.
- Preserve familiar product controls for forms, lists, and destructive actions.
- Use playful emphasis only where it supports motivation or feedback.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use clear contrast for text and controls, keyboard-accessible inputs and buttons, visible focus states, and reduced-motion fallbacks for nonessential animation. Avoid relying on color alone to communicate target, lock, completion, or reward status.
